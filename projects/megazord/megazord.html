Megazord Power Up - CAD Model Based on the Megazord's Sword

MIGHTY MORPHIN' POWER RANGERS!!

For a group project, my group decided to CAD the Megazord from Power Rangers. Evidently, I was assigned the sword.

From there, I simply used SolidWorks to model the sword, while searching up additional images to add more details to my model. By abusing surfacing tools such as lofts and knits, I was finally able to achieve the shape I wanted.
